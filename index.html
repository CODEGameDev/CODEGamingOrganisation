CODE Gaming Organisation
in short CO:GO (did you get the reference?) is a registered association started by students of the CODE University of Applied Sciences in Berlin. Our aim is to connect people from around the world through gaming and education. We are a team of passionate students who love gaming together and this project is a matter of our hearts - that is why we decided to found a registered association in order to provide you the best gaming experience with our own amazing Discord Server, our own dedicated servers for a variety of different games and with our vibrant and active community of passionate and amazing people from different places.
